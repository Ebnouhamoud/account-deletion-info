How to Delete Your Mariass Account

If you would like to permanently delete your account from the Mariass app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Mariass app on your device.
  2. Tap on Profile from the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and select Delete Account.
  4. Check the box labeled “I understand this is permanent and can't be undone.”
  5. Tap Delete Account to confirm.
Disclaimer: Deleting your account is a permanent action. All associated user data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
